What They Say About Being
Member of SRE ITS

“Becoming a member of ITS SRE is an opportunity to improve skills and knowledge about RE because the various trainings carried out are not boring, they are even more interesting to be listened to. And also the administrators are also friendly in accompanying members. Anyway, it's very useful starting from adding relationships because you can meet friends from other majors who also have an interest in RE and also of course increase understanding about the development of RE in the industry, both Indonesia and the world. Highly recommended for those who like RE issues!”.

- Aulia Amjad Lutfi
